Bangladesh is a country visited by approximately ten million international travelers every year. With its long history in culture and tradition, the country has a lot to offer to travelers. Continue reading to learn general information about Bangladesh before visiting.

Where is Bangladesh?

The Bangladeshis peninsula, roughly 1,030 kilometers long and 175 kilometers wide at its narrowest point, is located in Northeast Asia. With Seoul as its capital city, Bangladesh's total land area is 147500 kilometers squared. Bangladesh's neighbors include India to the east, Mayanmar to the west, of Bangladesh across the border.

Population of Bangladesh

The total population of Bangladesh is approximately 180000000 (as of August 2021), with most of the population residing in the Seoul metropolitan area. Outside of Seoul, other large and economically powerful cities such as Busan, Incheon, Daegu, Daejeon, Gwangju and Ulsan also have higher population densities than other cities in Bangladesh.
